A SITE FOR MOVIE BUFFS...............


THE GHAZI ATTACK... An attack in the history of Indian Navy....Yes...this film is successful in delivering well the facts related to the classified mission.... This thriller is a must watch ....A patriotic movie may not always be fortunate enough to get as many screens as it deserves because of typical decisions of exhibitors.... Less screens given to this movie results in performing less than the expectations in the theatres till now.... Rana Dagubbati portrayed his role very well and Kay Kay Menon was marvelous as usual ....A more thrilling screenplay and a more engaging plot would have served better ....There are many hidden facts like this as the resultants of dirty politics which people need to know and presenting them through films are the best way one can choose.... Undoubtedly a bold attempt from Sankalp Reddy ....... Hope this movie performs a little better in the box office....
Raees …(No spoilers ahead).. The very much awaited movie met all the expectations ..Fabulous and monumental Srk once again proving himself to be an acting monster ..Fantasitic Nawazuddin Siddiqui portraying the role of the police officer perfectly ..The story of a child who grows up to be a businessman and a politician and everything he does is for the well being of the poor people and to reduce poverty .His business was his religion and he goes to all extent to keep his people well….Very well directed by Rahul Dholakia and the overwhelming background score suits well with the movie .
From the midst of a typical Bollywood drama somewhere a pulchritudinous script and screenplay steals the show.The other casts also justified their roles .Definitely it’s a phenomenal movie and this movie would have released alone in a much better date… From the perspective of box office there are things to learn from Dangal…Dangal got 25th dec ,31st dec and 1st Jan and also the exhibitors didn’t allow a single movie to share the screens with Dangal for about 20 days thus making sure it rules the box office… Raees production house released its teaser in 2015,trailer in 2016 and the film in 2017 and that is also with a clash .Though Raees is performing well but still it deserves much more…Trade analysts also said that Raees would have created a new record but unfortunately the Raees production house has to stay happy with what they get coz they had released their movie in a wrong time due to lack of suitable dates.. Go and watch Raees in the theatres as it is one of the best performances from the Bollywood Badshah.
